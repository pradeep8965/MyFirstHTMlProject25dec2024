INDIA'S SPACE ODYSSEY

ACHIEVEMENTS OF ISRO

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is one of the most prestigious space agencies in the world. Founded in 1969, it has achieved numerous milestones in the realm of space exploration and technology.

ISRO has successfully launched numerous satellites and missions, including the historic Mangalyaan, also known as the Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM), which made India the first Asian nation to reach Martian orbit and the first nation globally to achieve it in the maiden attempt.
