MERA MADHYA PRADESH

Madhya Pradesh is a state located in central India.It is the Second-Largest State in our country.

M.P. with an area of 3,08,245square k.m.
The state is bound on the Nourth by UTTAR PRADESH,the East by CHHATTISGARH,the South by MAHARASHTRA and theWest by GUJRAT and RAJASTHAN.

There are 10 DIVISION and 55 DISTRICTS in Madhaya Pradesh.

  • Chhindwara is the largest district in M.P.
  • Niwari is the smallest district in M.P.

There are many Rivers in Madhaya Pradesh ,including the Narmada,Tapti Mahi Godavari and Chambal. NARMADA is called Ganga of Madhaya Pradesh.

The current Chief Minister of Madhaya Pradesh is Dr. Mohan Yadav.
He was Born on 25 March 1965 in Ujjain.
